About Us

We are the Trivia Titans team from the 3rd semester, Section C, Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the International Islamic University Chittagong (IIUC). Our project focuses on the creation of a news portal designed to disseminate university-related news to our fellow students. Under the guidance of our team president, Sanjida Sharmin, and continuous oversight and valuable advice from Eshrat Jahan Eshika, we have strived to enhance and refine our project for the benefit of our university community.
